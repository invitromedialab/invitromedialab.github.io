In Vitro Media
Temporary Soul Standing at the center of the continental shelf Biography

In Vitro, an artist duo co-founded by Xiyue Hu and Xing Xiao, engages in media art, focusing on the development of game engines, virtual reality, and augmented reality. Their creations, centered around biology, complex systems, and artificial intelligence, involve interdisciplinary collaborations with archaeologists and biologists. In Vitro holds the view that modern science and humanities are intertwined, not independent or irreducible fields. Utilizing game engines, they craft real-time simulations of complex systems mirroring ecosystems, and aim to broaden these explorations to encompass socio-economic systems, drawing parallels between the two.
