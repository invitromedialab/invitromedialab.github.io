Temporary Soul

Video game

2023
The game starts with a dialogue where one voice asserts that machines do not possess souls, and even if they do, they are unstable. However, another voice seems unconvinced by this claim. As the game unfolds, two players assume the roles of organisms with unstable souls, tasked with injecting different souls into these bodies to facilitate motion and advance the story.
